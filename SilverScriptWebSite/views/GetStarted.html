Reference the silverscript.js
http://silverscript.azurewebsites.net/scripts/silverscript.js
https://silverscript.azurewebsites.net/scripts/silverscript.js
or download the sample project to get the TypeScript definitions.


Set the datacontext
<div id="testDiv" data-context="/api/mycontroller">...</div>
or from javascript
$('testDiv').attributes["data-context-value"]=myObject;
or inside another datacontext
<div id="testDiv" data-context="{Binding Path=myProperty}">...</div>

Add your binding to your content (OneTime binding by default)
<Button data-binding="{Binding Path=name,Destination=Content}"> </Button>

Bind a different property
<a href="blank" data-binding="{Binding Path=name,Destination=href}"> My dynamic link </a>

Mix syntaxes
<div data-context="/viewmodels/mycontroller">
    <button data-binding="{Binding Path=name,Destination=Content}" data-context="/mycontroller/{DataBinding Path=id}"></button>
</div>
